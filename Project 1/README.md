# No-Show Appointments Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes the **No-Show Appointments dataset** to understand the factors that influence whether patients attend their medical appointments or miss them.

The goal is to identify patterns that may help predict patient attendance.

---

## Questions Explored
- Do different **age groups** show different attendance patterns?  
- Does **financial status (scholarship)** affect attendance?  
- Do **SMS reminders** improve attendance rates?  

---

## Key Findings
- Most patients are under 60 years old  
- Younger patients tend to attend appointments more frequently  
- Scholarship status does not show a strong impact on attendance  
- SMS reminders did not significantly improve attendance  

---

## Data Cleaning
- Renamed columns for consistency  
- Converted date columns to datetime format  
- Converted `no_show` column to numeric (0 = attended, 1 = missed)  
- No missing or duplicate values were found
